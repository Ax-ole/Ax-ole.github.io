Clash Royale

I am going to write information on the best mobile game in the world! You will want to play it I promise!

What can I say about Clash Royale besides that it is the most played tower defence, live and peer to peer game in existence. Ok I may be stretching this statement just a tad but regardless, I think it is very entertaining. Main concept is you start with a hand of cards if you will and every card has a unique character and stats. Seems basic enough right? Well it isâ€¦ Very much like chess and or even Yu-Gi-Oh. You have an elixir bar at the bottom of your screen that replenishes at a rate of 1 elixir a second. Then when you get to the last minute things go faster and faster. As the elixir now after being spent on summoning your card, now goes 2x faster. It can go up to 7x and that is called the legendary infinite elixir challenge.
